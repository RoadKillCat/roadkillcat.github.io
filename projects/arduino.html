ARDUINOS !!

Arduinos are microcontrollers that are programmed through a C based arduino language. Since they are so simple, there is a huge range of modules and stuff which you can integrate into stuf. You can find more out about them here. The arduino which I use mainly is the nano v3. It is based around the ATMEL ATmega328p chip which is also found on the uno board. It has a tiny form factor for what it can do which means that it can be mounted on projects easily. In addition, they are extremely CHEAP - I found a seller on ebay offering Â£1.13 for them!
